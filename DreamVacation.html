My Dream Vacation

https://eliu310011.github.io/dream-vacation/MapHub.html

This project serves as a mapping out of potential tourist locations chosen by the user. Using google maps and knowledge of the destination, we were able to chart visible travel markers and lines indicating directions of travel, length of travel, and pictures of the designated locations.
